Graphic Design

La idea, una letra, unas palabra, entretejen el camino, mutan en los viajes y se enamoran, se odian tras la traicion y una de ellas desaparece al final del puente...

A long-life sketcher, with lead and ink, then it comes the "clickities" and keyboard-smashing. For over nine years I have been working as a designer, experienced creating fresh and pretty logotypes, corporate identity stuff and I usually brag about having plenty understand of typography behavior and its facets. I have developed over the years the skill of spotting pixels out of place and some other minimal details of the same sort. I have a strong belief that there are no universal solutions and as far as my work goes, even when this is might be the messiest part it, it is also the most rewarding.

Editorial Desgin

Text-box can be seen as an image on paper or display, it catches the eye, then the story wants to be told, requires the reader´s attention, the air surrounding the box and the air between lines and types, all of this is part of the so called the "image" of text. Ordered as a whole, the ink or pixel, fills the page as liquid and as long as it si poured into, it takes the shape of the container, the container is what as a designer, I can control and mould, no random shapes.

Web Development

The thing I love most about web design, is that there is never too much information to display, it has all the space it needs, as long as there is an ordered readability there are no limitations.

Absolute control whit a couple of variables, sounds simple, but these variables explode in the backgound. I am more focused in interactivity, make the ball to bounce, make it float, make it fly, make it burst and reveal a tiny mouse driving a robot... why not?
